01. About Me

Hi, my name is Ikjoon Park. I am a third year Computer Science student at the University of California San Diego. I've had an interest in Computer Science at an early age due to my father's occupation as then the lead animator at Rockstar Studios, a video game publisher. Growing up, I was naturally drawn to videogames and continued this hobby throughout my highschool career. My interest in video games eventually amalgamated into a general interest in technology and computer programming, and I chose to pursue this academically through an university setting.
